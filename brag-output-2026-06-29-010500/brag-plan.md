# Brag Plan: Expense Tracker

## What is this app?
An offline-first PWA that tracks personal income & expenses in ₹ — local-first (Dexie) with Supabase cloud sync, per-category budgets, multi-account (HDFC / GPay / Paytm), and undo on every delete. A genuinely humble personal-finance app.

## The angle
yc-parody: treat this modest ₹ expense tracker as if it just closed a $40M Series A. Deliver world-changing fintech-disruptor gravitas over a screen that is, in fact, just someone logging ₹240 on chai. The joke is the dead-straight seriousness — boardroom voice, absurd stakes, tiny real product. The product shown is 100% real (real palette, real ₹L/₹K formatting, real Cash Balance card).

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
Black screen, emerald cursor. One deadpan line slams in: **"The personal finance crisis is over."** Beat. Sub-line: *"We built a tracker."* The mismatch between the claim and the modesty is the hook.

## Key moments (the middle)
- **Onboarding "Welcome!"** — Quick Add chips light up one by one: HDFC 8682, GPay, Paytm. Framed as "onboarding institutional partners."
- **Add a transaction** — the real Add screen: ₹ symbol, amount types in (₹240), Expense toggle, a category chip selected. Framed as "executing a transaction."
- **Dashboard reveal** — the real Cash Balance gradient card + Income/Expense + Today/Txns stats + budget ring filling. Framed as "real-time treasury intelligence."

## Outro / punchline
Logotype "Expense Tracker" with a fake tagline that overclaims one last time: **"Series A. Allegedly."** then **"You built it. ₹240 at a time."**

## User flow worth showing
entry → key action → result:
1. Onboarding: pick accounts (HDFC / GPay / Paytm chips) → "Start Tracking"
2. Add Transaction: ₹240, Expense, category chip → "Add Expense"
3. Dashboard: Cash Balance card updates, budget ring fills, Today stat ticks.

## Audio direction
- Role: sparse professional accents over a quiet confident bed (yc-parody = restrained).
- Music: bundled "Happy Beats / Business Moves" (ende.app) — deadpan corporate-optimistic, perfect parody fit.
- Music treatment: start ~0.3s, low bed (~-18dB feel), gentle fade-out under outro. No big drops.
- Music cue guidance: track has a bundled cue preset in assets/music/cues/. Target 1 strong cue at the Dashboard reveal (~13s). Keep accents minimal elsewhere. Restraint note: yc-parody — fewer, quieter cues; let the deadpan breathe.
- Audio-reactive treatment: subtle; let the Cash Balance card presence breathe gently on the bed, no waveform bars.
- SFX posture: sparse, motion-matched — soft key ticks on the ₹ amount typing, a single clean chip-select click, one soft tick when the budget ring lands.
- Audio-coupled moments: ₹ amount typing, account chips lighting one-by-one, budget ring fill.
- Restraint rule: no airhorns, no riser whooshes, no hype stingers. The comedy is dryness.
