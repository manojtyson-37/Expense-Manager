🚀 *Expense Tracker — What's New (v1.3)*

Big update! Here's everything that shipped:

💰 *Money tracking*
• Salary/recurring income can now be added as a subscription (not just expenses)
• Loans can have a due date — get a reminder when one's overdue
• Set a savings goal, track progress toward it, see it on your dashboard

📊 *See your spending better*
• New Trends page — income vs expense chart, last 6 months
• See your top spending categories broken down month by month
• Get a heads-up when a transaction looks unusually large for that category

⚡ *Faster to use*
• Auto-categorize rules — e.g. "Swiggy" always → Food, automatically
• Copy last month's budgets in one tap
• Budgets can roll over unused (or overspent) amount into next month
• Attach a photo of a receipt to any transaction
• Get notified (in-app) when a loan's overdue, a subscription's renewing soon, or you've hit a budget limit

🔒 *More reliable*
• Every edit now works properly offline — categories, accounts, budgets included
• Backup export/import now covers everything (budgets, subscriptions, loans, goals — not just transactions)
• Can now import transactions from a CSV file, not just export

That's the whole list 🎉 Let me know if anything looks off!
